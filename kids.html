How to help the birds in your Garden

With the growth of the economy an the concreate jungles of the City it leads to a lot of our animal friends being forced out of their home. this makes it a lot harder for them to repreoduce as their ecosystems are often destroyed with the development of new urban zones. In efforts to aim our feathered friends Wilson Zoo have found terrific ways in which we can help our Animal friends.

The Bird Feeder

Birds can eat up to 10,000 calories a day, they spend most of the day looking for food for their young and consuming food. The Bird Bath

As birds have waterproof feathers they don't actually use Birth Bath's to bathe. They use it to release dirt that gets stuck in their feathers. This allows for a much more fluid movement when flying around. They also use the bird bath as a drinking water. Click here to learn about 10 Easy DIY Birth Bath Projects you can make at home.

The Bird House

A Bird House offers Birds a safe area to lay their eggs without having to worry about potential predators stealing them. This help keep out potential threats to the bird. It also gives them a safe space to rest at night time comapred to their traiditional home of nests made out of twigs and pieces of grass.
