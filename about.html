About Us

On September 1st 1985,  Wilson Zoo first opened its doors.  It was founded with a simple goal in mind; to bring together fellow animal lovers and enthusiasts.

 

In 1985 Wilson Zoo took in a number of rescued animals from all around the globe. From dying ecosystems ruined in the Rio De Jeniero Rainforest to the Sub-Saharan mammals in the African Desert. Wilson Zoo aims to make it a welcoming home for all of its occupants. Wilson Zoo aims to be a safe haven for different types of endangered species to come together and rejuvenate their race 

Wilson Zoo has expended tremendously over the years. It is now 20 hectares in size and attracts millions of animal lovers world wide through out the year. We offer a range of packages and memberships to all our visitors, so that you can always feel as part of our lovely family no matter where you are in the world. 

Wilson Zoo is run by compassionate zookeepers who dedicate their life to animal health and care. They understand the creatures' natural habitats, food, genetics, animal health, and characteristic traits

At Wilson zoo we make sure to follow all the rules and regulations when it comes to the practice of animal welfare. We adhere by the European and Global Codes of Conduct creating partnerships with other institutions and agencies to help enhance experience for our animals and our visitors . Rest assured we do thorough checks on who were partner with to ensure that they adhere to our strict rules and regulations to make sure they are up to standard with the European and Global Codes of Conduct.

Our Convservation Work

Many endangered species with small natural populations may be found at Wilson Zoo. We work directly with World Land and Trust to give financing and assistance to them in their aim to safeguard species from extinction, both in the UK and abroad.
