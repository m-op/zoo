Donations and Special Memberships

How your donations help Us?

At Wilson Zoo we are happy and veyr greatful for any contributions anyone wishes to donate. No matter how big or small, we utlise all donations to ensure that our animal friends at the Zoo and around the world reap every penny fit on your contributions. We recognise that the previous few years have been difficult for everyone, therefore we ask that individuals donate only what they can. We appreciate every donation tremendously no matter how big or small!

 

Donations & Convservation Work

We take a portion of all donations and use it ot aid our conservation partners World Land Trust in their mission. They have amazon core values which falls aligns with our beliefs here at Wilson Zoo. We work together with them to ensure animals all over the world get the help they need.
